Style Guide 1.0

Purpose/Theme

Our Zen Garden theme has a watermelon look to it. We have a mix of green and red shades. The objective of this Style Guide is to show the different styles of elements within our Zen Garden Website. We are using SCSS as a preprocessor of CSS. We have organized the SCSS files into different sections. The style-guide.scss imports all the partial files to create one css.
